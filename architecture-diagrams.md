# Storytime Platform Diagram Pack

This file pack contains the platform diagrams in two formats:

- `architecture-diagrams.html`
  - Open in a browser.
  - Use the built-in `Download SVG` buttons for email-ready attachments.
  - Use `Print to PDF` to export the full pack as one document.
- `storytime-master-architecture.mmd`
- `storytime-runtime-flow.mmd`
- `storytime-security-architecture.mmd`
- `storytime-data-domain.mmd`
- `storytime-ops-tooling.mmd`
  - Raw Mermaid source files for editing or re-exporting in Mermaid Live, GitHub, or docs tooling.

## Recommended Email Workflow

1. Open `architecture-diagrams.html` in your browser.
2. Click `Download SVG` on the diagrams you want to attach.
3. Attach the exported `.svg` files to your email.

## Recommended Single-Document Workflow

1. Open `architecture-diagrams.html`.
2. Click `Print to PDF`.
3. Save and send the PDF.
